# Future Roadmap

This document describes potential future capabilities for Jirita beyond the MVP.

Items listed here are not part of the initial release and should not influence MVP decisions.

The primary objective is to deliver an exceptional core experience before expanding the platform.

---

# Version 2 (Near-Term Expansion)

## Client Portal

Allow external clients to access selected projects.

Capabilities may include:

- View project status
- View milestones
- View assigned deliverables
- View reports
- Submit requests
- Comment on tickets

### Goal

Increase transparency while reducing communication overhead.

---

## Executive Dashboard

Provide a high-level overview of organizational activity.

Potential metrics:

- Active projects
- Active milestones
- Open tickets
- Completed work
- Team workload
- Time utilization

### Goal

Allow business owners and managers to understand project health at a glance.

---

## GitHub Integration

Connect projects and tickets to source control activity.

Potential capabilities:

- Link repositories
- Associate commits with tickets
- Associate pull requests with tickets
- Display deployment status
- View repository activity

### Goal

Improve visibility between project management and software delivery.

---

## Notifications

Provide real-time updates across the platform.

Potential channels:

- In-app notifications
- Email notifications
- Slack integration
- Microsoft Teams integration

### Goal

Keep users informed without requiring constant manual checking.

---

# Version 3 (AI-Powered Features)

## AI Ticket Creation

Allow users to create tickets using natural language.

Examples:

- Create a ticket from a message.
- Create a ticket from meeting notes.
- Create a ticket from a bug report.

### Goal

Reduce administrative work.

---

## AI Project Summaries

Generate automatic summaries for:

- Projects
- Milestones
- Tickets
- Weekly activity

### Goal

Improve visibility and communication.

---

## AI Risk Detection

Identify potential project risks automatically.

Examples:

- Delayed milestones
- Overloaded team members
- Long-running tickets
- Blocked work

### Goal

Help teams identify problems before they become critical.

---

# Version 4 (Team Management)

## Capacity Planning

Provide visibility into team allocation and availability.

Potential capabilities:

- Capacity forecasting
- Allocation tracking
- Availability planning

### Goal

Improve project planning and staffing decisions.

---

## Skills Matrix

Maintain a centralized view of team skills and expertise.

Potential capabilities:

- Skill catalog
- Competency tracking
- Team expertise mapping

### Goal

Improve staffing and project assignments.

---

## Staffing Management

Support resource allocation across projects.

Potential capabilities:

- Team assignments
- Staffing recommendations
- Project demand forecasting

### Goal

Optimize team utilization across the organization.

---

# Guiding Principle

Every future feature must align with Jirita's core philosophy:

- Simplicity over complexity
- Usability over configurability
- Focus over feature quantity

No future capability should compromise the speed, clarity, or ease of use that define the platform.
